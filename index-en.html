Frequently asked questions

Do I need to edit theme code?

No. The app uses Theme Editor blocks and sections, including Product Feedback and Meta Social Carousel, plus a storefront embed for promo badges and discount pricing.

Can campaigns target collections and tags as well as products?

Yes. The app logic supports products, collections and tags, together with start/end dates and centralized campaign management.

What kinds of feedback can shoppers leave?

Three modes are available: stars, thumbs up/down and hearts. The app also shows aggregated product-level sentiment stats.

How is the Instagram feed powered?

The module supports Meta/Instagram API settings, manual mode and external proxy/JSON feed mode, depending on the merchant setup.

Can campaigns be imported in bulk?

Yes. The Promotions module includes CSV import/export actions for faster merchandising and seasonal campaign work.
