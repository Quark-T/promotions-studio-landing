# Shopify App Store Optimization

Verified against the live Shopify App Store redirect `https://quarkdev.agency/ps` on `2026-05-13`.

## Current blockers

- The live App Store title already leans toward discounts and badges, but the visible app name is `Promotions badge e IG Carousel`. That weakens brand consistency versus `Promotions Studio`.
- The first-screen promise is too broad. It makes the app look like a generic Instagram/feed tool instead of a promotions app with extra modules.
- The live body copy contains the typo `Instagam`.
- Search ads are currently too broad and drift into Shopify-native intent that does not match the app.

## Recommended positioning

Primary acquisition wedge:
`Scheduled discounts + custom product badges for Shopify`

Brand:
`Promotions Studio`

Secondary modules:

- `Product Feedback`
- `Meta Social Carousel`

## Paste-ready listing copy

### App name

`Promotions Studio`

### Short value proposition

`Schedule Shopify discounts and make them visible with custom product badges.`

### Description opener

`Promotions Studio helps Shopify merchants schedule discounts across products, collections and tags, then surface those offers with fully customizable product badges and compare-at pricing.`

### Supporting sentence

`Product Feedback and Meta Social Carousel are included as secondary modules for merchants who also want product-page trust signals and social proof from the same app.`

### Bullet order

1. `Schedule discount campaigns with start and end dates`
2. `Target products, collections and tags from one admin workflow`
3. `Show custom product badges and compare-at prices`
4. `Import and export promotions via CSV`
5. `Add Product Feedback blocks from Theme Editor`
6. `Add Meta Social Carousel as optional social proof`

## Screenshot order

1. `Campaign dashboard`
2. `Discounted pricing and badge visibility`
3. `Product Feedback`
4. `Meta Social Carousel`

## Ads implication

- Keep core search around `discounts`, `badges` and `compare-at price` intent.
- Pause or demote `instagram feed app` style terms until the promotions-first message proves it can win clicks and installs.
- Expand into feedback and social proof only after the core positioning starts producing qualified traffic.
